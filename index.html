About Us

The Creative Inc. is a team of talented Ashesi students founded and based in Ashesi.

Creative is one of the few services and applications developed by the company. The Creative is a data and visually driven web application for creatives worldwide to showcase their talents, along the spaces of arts, writing and entertainment and also serving as a safe space for marketing talents and exploring sales potential of designed artefacts.

The vision of The Creative is to connect Creatives no matter the geographical location and to create a conducive space for them to grow their potential.

The Problem

Because of piracy, artists are unable to profit from their skills. Additionally, it is challenging for people to identify a site specifically for artworks demonstration and display. Many artists attempt to use social media sites like LinkedIn, but because these sites are multipurpose, their artworks are buried beneath other posts.

The Solution

Creative Inc. is developing a data powered website to help creatives connect, showcase and market their talents without worrying about piracy.
